About the Placement Cell

The Placement Cell at Techno Main Salt Lake is dedicated to facilitating students in achieving their career goals. The cell actively organizes campus interviews, resume workshops, aptitude training, and industry interaction programs. We have established relationships with top corporate houses to ensure excellent placement opportunities for our students.

Our recruiters include major players in tech, consulting, finance, and more. Our infrastructure supports smooth and organized placement drives with modern facilities.
